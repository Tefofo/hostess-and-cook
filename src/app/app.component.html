About Me

Who am i?

Hello,

My name is Andiswa Skweza.

I am not a Chef but a Cook, I started self-teaching how to cook and bake from an early age of 7\8yrs old – by observing my mother and uncle in the kitchen when they prepared food. Growing up, cooking was not a duty\chore I was expected to do after school or during school holidays, so I kept trying out dishes when no one was watching and would wait for them to be surprised by my food when they get home.

Since then, anything involving food and hosting family and friends gives me great joy, and absolutely stems from my voracious appetite as a child. I have always loved food. When I’m not eating, I’m thinking about food, when I’m not thinking about food I’m reading and researching about food.

I love experimenting with different combinations of flavours and ingredients. I am always planning the next great meal to cook or treat to bake. As a home cook I like to believe that every dish I make is a personal expression, with each ingredient being specially selected. I strive to put emotion into my cooking, and I believe this reflects in the taste of the dishes that I prepare. I realize that not every combination I try will be a success, but this is one of the benefits of cooking, I can just wash the dishes, and try again.

I cannot think of anything that gives me greater joy and pleasure than cooking for family and friends. Food manages to bring people together, in a way that few other things can. Sitting around a table with loved ones, sharing stories and interacting over a delicious meal is particularly important to me – because I truthfully see and experience food as a medium of communication.

Nowadays we share people’s attention with Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Whatsapp and emails. It is my ultimate goal that when I cook and prepare food that will give you no choice but to enjoy yourself wholeheartedly and disconnect from the world, except of course if you want to post a picture of the meal from Hostess and Cook for your friends to see.

About us

What Do we Do?

Hostess and Cook is a home-based cooking and hosting innovative company that aims to make cooking and hosting easy for its customers. We tailor make the food according to our customers' taste buds and dietary requirements. We cook food from South African traditional cuisines (sometimes with a twist) to some of the world's famous cuisines.

Hostess and Cook values healthy lifestyle and living, thus you can trust that our cooking has your (and family) best interest at heart. Our team understands and always remembers that while we are better today than yesterday that will not always be the case. Our search to deliver excellent food and services is boundless.

Due to popular demand, our business now provides cooking classes for our interested customers. We teach South Africans how to cook the best way we know how. And also offer this service to our international customers seeking to learn the South African way of cooking or looking for bonding extramural activities while visiting our beautiful country. This service also extends to corporate team building activities.
